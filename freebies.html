Shopmium

Shopmium is a cashback app which has offers on a number of products at popular shops. Simply download the app and find an offer that interests you. Buy the product from one of the participating stores, and then scan the receipt using the app. Your cashback will be sent to your bank account or paypal within a few days. CheckoutSmart

Much like Shopmium, CheckoutSmart is a cashback app. Simply purchase an eligible item from a participating shop, and then scan the receipt using the app. One drawback of CheckoutSmart is that the minimum payout threshold is £5, and if the payout is less than £20 you will be charged a 5% fee. Therefore make you sure you can commit to using the app for making multiple purchases before you start. Only "free" full cashback items are shown in the table.
